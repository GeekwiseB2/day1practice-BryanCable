Bryan

It all started with a Foo Fighters concert.

I was having some pre-concert drinks and tacos across the street with some friends. I was getting a bit chatty and started talking about my desire to start learning to code and possibly enroll in one of the very expensive coding camps. One of the friends mentioned that the building he works in actually offers a few courses. A few days later I was on my Harley and headed to Bitwise!

The moment I walked into the building I knew that Bitwise was a place that I wanted to be involved with. It was like nothing I had ever seen in the central valley before. I proceeded to follow the signs painted on the floor down the stairs and into Geekwise. This is where I happened upon Ryeker who, after telling me his story, convinced me that this is where I would begin me next path.

After six weeks of Websites for Beginners I felt that I had a pretty good grasp on HTML and CSS structure and was ready for more. I am literally dreaming about code at this point. I am excited to learn more and am willing to put in the work to do so.
